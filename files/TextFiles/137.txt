List of Wikimedia Commons shortcuts for English pages (Commons — Categories). CAT: is an alias for Category: (see Help:Namespaces), CT: is a not (yet) very popular prefix for shortcuts to important Commons talk pages. COM: is actually an alias for Commons: or Project:, e.g., COM:COM and Project:COM arrive on Commons:COM, where they are redirected to this page Commons:Shortcuts. Likewise COM:Shortcuts and Project:Shortcuts go to this page (without redirection).
